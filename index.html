About ScopeFace

ScopeFace is a digital stethoscope designed for modern care. By combining smart hardware with an intuitive app, it turns auscultation into a powerful, repeatable tool.

Designed for precision

Our microphone isolates key clinical frequencies to capture the sounds that matter. Every decision, from audio path to materials, is focused on clarity and reliability.

Built for modularity

ScopeFace is housed in a durable, lightweight shell designed for future growth. Its modular build supports upgrades like ML analysis and custom PCBs.

Centered on usability

Our iOS app was designed with clinicians in mind. Simple flows, clean visuals, and secure access make it easy to tag, take notes, and replay recordings on the go.

Our Process

We built ScopeFace through research, iteration, and clinical feedback. From selecting components to refining design, each step brought us closer to a tool that works where it counts.

User Research

Our conversations with clinicians highlighted a clear need: a simple, affordable way to amplify and replay heart and lung sounds. Every step of our design process was shaped directly by these insights.

Signal Capture & Testing

We started with clinicians' essential needs, sourcing a microphone precisely tuned to heart and lung sounds. Testing ensured reliability and clarity in diverse clinical settings.

Integrated Audio Pipeline

Our secure pipeline smoothly transfers audio from device to app, clearly labeling and safely storing each recording for instant, uncompromised playback.

Companion Interface

Built in SwiftUI, our app offers an elegant and intuitive experience, making recording, note-taking, tagging, and playback seamless. Clinicians can effortlessly manage and revisit sessions with clarity.

Iterative Hardware Design

We designed and tested a 3D-printed shell for usability and durability. Feedback from early users shaped our design, prioritizing comfort, durability, and ease of use in every iteration.

Seamless Auth & Storage

Secure user authentication and organized data storage via Firebase ensures each recording is easily traceable, protected, and conveniently linked to clinician profiles.
